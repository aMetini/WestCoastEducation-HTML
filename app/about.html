Mission

WestCoast Education serves the educational needs of our diverse local and global community. The school empowers students to achieve their educational goals by providing high quality and innovative programs and services leading to academic degrees, college transfers, certificates in career and technical education, basic skills, and workforce development to enable lifelong learning. The college promotes student learning and development through fostering a respectful, supportive, participatory, and equitable campus climate of student engagement and academic inquiry.

Transforming Lives

Talent is universal, but opportunities are not. With access to online learning resources and instruction, anyone, anywhere, can gain skills, and transform their lives in meaningful ways.
